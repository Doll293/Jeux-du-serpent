Project Overview

Welcome to our Snake Game Project!
This classic arcade-style game brings a nostalgic twist to modern gaming.
The Snake Game is a simple yet addictive experience that challenges players to control a snake as it maneuvers through a dynamic environment, consuming food and growing in length.
Our project aims to capture the essence of this iconic game, offering an engaging and entertaining experience for players of all ages.

Key Features:
Classic Gameplay: Experience the timeless joy of controlling a snake to collect food and grow. Scoring System: Compete with friends and track your progress with a built-in scoring system.

Join us in reliving the magic of the Snake Game! Whether you're a seasoned gamer or a newcomer, our project offers a delightful blend of nostalgia and excitement.
