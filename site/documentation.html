Step 2: Run the executable.
Step 3: Have fun with this game.

3. Gameplay

Get ready for an addictive gaming experience:

Objective: Control the snake to consume food items and grow in length.
Challenge: Avoid collisions with the snake's own body and the game borders.
Scoring: Earn points for each food item consumed. The longer you survive, the higher your score.
